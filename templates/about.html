Frequently Asked

Questions & Answers

What is your minimum order quantity?

Our standard MOQ is 500 metres per fabric type per colour. For custom Jacquard designs the MOQ is 1,000 metres. We also offer sample lengths of 5–10 metres for quality assessment before bulk orders.

How long does production take?

Standard weaves take 15–21 business days from order confirmation. Custom Jacquard and special finishes may take 25–35 days. Rush orders (7–10 days) are available at a surcharge for in-stock grey fabric orders.

Do you offer custom dyeing and finishing?

Yes. We offer Pantone-matched yarn dyeing, piece dyeing, and a full range of finishes including anti-wrinkle, mercerisation, and flame retardant treatments. Our zero-discharge facility ensures environmentally compliant dyeing.

What payment terms do you accept?

For new clients: 50% advance, 50% before dispatch. For verified long-term partners we offer Net 30/60 terms via LC or TT. We accept all major international wire transfers and trade finance instruments.
