Before You Write

Common Questions

What is your minimum order quantity?

Standard MOQ is 500 metres per fabric type. Custom Jacquard patterns require a minimum of 1,000 metres. Sample cuts of 5–10 metres are available free of charge.

How long before I get a response?

We target a response within 24 business hours, typically faster. For urgent requirements, WhatsApp is the fastest channel — our team is available Mon–Sat 9am–7pm IST.

Can I visit the factory before ordering?

Absolutely. Mill visits are welcome and encouraged. Please use the form above or WhatsApp to schedule your visit — we'll arrange a guided tour of the weaving, dyeing, and QC facilities.
